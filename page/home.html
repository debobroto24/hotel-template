Owned by a member of the Thai nobility, this picturesque resort is set in the grounds of the owners family home. Tucked away in a quiet soi the resort is close to the town center. From here you are walking distance to the daily Night Market as well as the weekend market. Baan Laksasubha offers several excursions depending on what you are looking to experience: culture or adventure. Best would be a combination of the two, in order to take in all that Hua-Hin has to offer. A must visit after a day of sightseeing is the on-site Zaanti Spa, which includes signature treatments formulated by the owner. With just 16, two storey villas, you are guaranteed privacy, while the scenic setting makes for a memorable holiday. Baan Laksasubha Resort is a true boutique property that is sure to win your heart with the first look.

LOCATION

Tucked away from the main road, Baan Laksasubha is conveniently located in the center of Hua Hin and minutes away from various outlets such as the night market, coffee shops and restaurants. There are several ways to get to Hua Hin. Scheduled flights and daily trains from Bangkok to Hua Hin are available. Air-conditioned buses leave the Southern Bus Terminal every 30 minutes. Driving by car from Bangkok takes about two and a half hours.
